Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Honeycomb Vape & Smoke located?

We have two Miami-area locations: 20302 S Dixie Hwy in Cutler Bay, FL 33189 and 11326 Quail Roost Dr in Miami, FL 33157. Both are open Monday through Sunday, 10 AM to 10 PM.

What brands of disposable vapes do you carry?

We carry a wide selection of disposable vapes including Flum, Fume, HQD USA, Off Stamp, and many more. We stay on top of the latest releases and restock regularly so you always have access to the newest products.

Do you sell hookahs and hookah accessories?

Yes! We have a great selection of hookahs at various price points and colors, plus all the accessories you need including coals, hoses, bowls, and premium shisha tobacco.

Do you carry Delta 8 and CBD products?

Absolutely. We stock a full range of Delta 8 products and CBD items including gummies, tinctures, cartridges, flower, and topicals from trusted, lab-tested brands.

What glass pieces do you have available?

We carry an extensive selection of glass pipes, water pipes, rigs, and custom blown pieces from top brands like Grav, Piranha, and Santa Cruz. Our customers consistently say our glass prices are the best in the area.

What are your hours of operation?

Both of our locations are open 7 days a week, Monday through Sunday from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. We're here when you need us.

Do you carry rolling papers and wraps?

Yes, we have a huge selection of rolling papers and wraps from top brands including Raw, Backwoods, Grabba Leaf, Blazy Susan, Dutch Masters, and many more. If it rolls, we carry it.

Is the shop wheelchair accessible? Can I bring my dog?

Yes to both! Both of our locations are fully wheelchair accessible and dog-friendly. We also have bike parking available.
